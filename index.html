Explore Our Menu

Mocha

A mocha is a Latte with a shot of chocolate, making it a coffee twist on hot chocolate.

Cafe Latte

A caffe latte is a very mild, creamy espresso drink milk with a large amount of streamed milk and topped with a thin layer of foam.

Cappuccino

The cappuccino is an italian espresso drink layered in equal parts of espresso, streamed milk, and a thick, frothy milk foam.


Flat White

The Flate White is the strong espresso drink balance by a thin, valvety layer of streamed milk.

Cortado

A cortado is made with roughly equal parts espreeso and streamed milk.

Espresso

The cafe specializes in crafting highly concentrated espreeso shots.
